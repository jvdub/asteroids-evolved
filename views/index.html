How To Play

You're the commander of a ship in deep space. You've just entered a very dangerous area that has a large number of asteroids. What's worse: it's infested with aliens. Your crew is counting on you to guide them through the asteroid field safely. You must avoid colliding with any asteroids and prevent them from colliding with your ship. If any one of these asteroids damages your ship, all your lives will be in grave danger.

  • Use thrust to move the ship in the direction it is facing
  • Rotate the ship to change the direction the ship is facing
  • Fire missiles at asteroids to destroy them
  • Beware of alien ships!
